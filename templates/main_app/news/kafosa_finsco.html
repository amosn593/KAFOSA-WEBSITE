KAFOSA PARTNERS WITH FINSCO AFRICA TO PROVIDE AFFORDABLE HOUSES TO IT'S MEMBERS

KAFOSA has partnered with FINSCO AFRICA to provide affordables houses to it's members, under a program named RENT TO OWN.

Members will be required to make a small deposit, and make the rest of the payment through monthly rental payments, as they occupy the houses.

Members will be provided with loan facilities through their respective saccos.

The houses consists of one bedroom, two bedrooms, three bedrooms apartments and bunglows built using latest technology and are environmental friendly.
